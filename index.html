Who I Am

I'm a CS and Finance student at Rutgers, graduating May 2028. I like building things that sit at the intersection of software and money — projects where the code actually matters and the output isn't just a homework assignment.

I spent last summer at a Mumbai company writing Java tools that automated their pricing and cut down a ton of manual errors. Before that I built Asset Universe, a full-stack investment simulator I still actively work on. I enjoy the whole process — picking up a new API, figuring out the architecture, deploying it, and iterating from there.
